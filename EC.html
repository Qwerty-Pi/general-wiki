Elliptic Curve

Definition

Curve $C$ (genus 1): $y^2 + a_1 xy + a_3 y = x^3 + a_2 x^2 + a_4 x + a_6$.

Short Weierstrass: $y^2 = x^3 + ax + b$

Montegomery Form: $By^2 = x^3 + Ax^2 + x$

Forumula

Hyperelliptic Curve

Divisor Group $\text{Div}(C)$: Free Group over Points on Curve $C$. Elements in form of $\sum_{P \in C} C_P [P]$ with $C_P \in \mathbb{Z}$ (finite nonzero $C_P$).

Degree $d$: $\sum_{P \in C} C_P$. Elements with degree $0$ forms subgroup $\text{Div}^{0}(C)$.

Divisor of function $f$: $C_P := $ degree of zeros minus degree of poles. Principal divisor in $\text{Div}^{P}(C)$ means a divisor of a function.

Jacobian $J(C)$: Quotient Group $\text{Div}^{0}(C) / \text{Div}^{P}(C)$. Mumfold representation. [source]
