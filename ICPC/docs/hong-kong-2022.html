Hong Kong Regional 2022

A. Minimise the number of registers needed to create a tree with parent pointers.

B. Find expected number of connected components in a $(N + 1) \times (M + 1)$ grid, where $(i, 1 \dots j)$ painted for $p_{i, j}$ probability and $(1 \dots i, j)$ painted for $q_{i, j}$ probability.

C. Paint a $N \times M$ grid with $\frac{N \times M}{2}$ black and white cells such that all rows and all columns pairwise distinct.

D. Shortest path with bi-colour edges with different weights in queries. $O((n + q) \cdot n^{2/3})$

E. Given array $A$ of length $N$. Count subarray where each value appears not equal to $K$ times. $O(N \log N)$

F. Given $N$ non-zero digits, insert $K$ ($\le 6$) '+' to minimise expression. $O(\frac{3^K}{K + 1} \cdot N)$

G. Calculate area spanned by two segments of limited angle.

H. Output $\lceil \frac{l}{b} \rceil \cdot b \cdot k$.

I. Given $N$ points $P_1, P_2, \dots, P_N$. Given $Q$ queries to find the closest pair of points in $P_{L, \cdots, R}$. Binary Lattice in $O(N \log N \log V + Q \log N)$.

J. Find $\sum_{x = 0}^{N - 1} \max(x, \frac{1}{n} \sum_{y = 0}^{N - 1} x \oplus y)$. Continuous ranges.

K. Given array $A$ of length $N$. Operation: $A_i \rightarrow 1 \cdots \lfloor \frac{A_i - 1}{2} \rfloor$. Maximise GCD.

L. Given permutation $A$ of length $N$ and $M$ operations to delete maximum element in subarray of length $l_i$. Determine if can turn $A$ to $B$.
