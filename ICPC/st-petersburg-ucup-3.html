Universal Cup 3 Stage 1: St. Petersburg

A. Given permutation of length $N$ ($\le 5 \times 10^4$). Find # of pairs $(i, j)$ such that $s_{i+l} \lt s_{j+l}$ simulatenously holds for $0 \le l \lt M$. $O(\frac{N^2}{w})$ bitset.

B: Given regular polygon of $N$ vertices. Operation: Pick three points and add parallelogram point. Given a list and determine if form regular polygon. Do in $\mathbb{F}_p$.

C: Given an array of length $N$. Operation: Remove element. Determine max range sum.

D: Given $0 \le X \lt 2N$. Protocol: query $T$, return if $X \le T \lt X + N$. Determine $X$ in $2\sqrt{N} - \epsilon$ queries.

E: Given $N$ and $K$. Find max # of coins $C$ such that you know the fake lightest coin with $N$ weighs, at most $K$ lies and $3K + 1$ guesses. Output $\ln f(N, K)$ and off by at most 10 in $\tilde O(1)$. Approximation and potential.

F: Find minimial degree polynomial that contains $N$ ($\le 30$) given points and is $\mathbb{Z} \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}$.

G: Find non-intersecting Hamilton paths in graph with parameter $(N = 10^4, M = 2 \times 10^5, K = 8)$. Greedy and remove.

H: Output $f(N)$ where $f$ is some easy function.

I: Given $\texttt{S}$ and $\texttt{R}$ which are spin and rotate. Find inverse of minimal length.

J: Given $N = 2n$ elements of sum $2 \times 10^9$. Split them into two sets of $n$ elements and sum $10^9$.

K: Given a graph. Print the graph.

L: Given $a \ge b, c$. Increase one value at a time and first element must be max. Count # of ways in $O(a^2)$.

M: Given cyclic toilets. Simulate assignment of toilets.

N: Run-twice task. Convert labelled graph of size of $N$ to unlabelled graph of size $N + \lceil \log_2 N \rceil + 3$ and convert back.

O: Given $X_{i+2} = A \cdot X_{i+1} + B \cdot X_i$, $X_1$ and $X_N$. Construct sequence.
