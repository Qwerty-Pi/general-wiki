LCG (Linear Congruence Generator)

Definition

Parameters: $p, a, b$.

Generated Numbers: $(x_n)_{n \ge 0}$ where $x_n = (a x_{n - 1} + b) \mod p$.

Well-known Attacks

Stern's Attack

"Linear relation among the vectors involving partially known bits implies a linear relation among the vectors involving all of the bits" [Source].

Let lattice $\mathcal{L} = \{\vec{W_i}\}_{1 \le i \le N}$ where $\vec{V_i} \sim \vec{W_i}$. Then construct a short orthogonal vector $\vec{\lambda}$ such that $\sum_{i = 1}^{n} \lambda_i \vec{V_i} = \vec{0}$ with LLL.

Claim: $\vec{U} = \sum_{i = 1}^{n} \lambda_i \vec{W_i} = 0$ as well, since $\vec{U} \in \mathcal{L}$ (implies $\vec{U}$ is $\vec{0}$ or sufficiently long) and $\vec{U}$ is short since $\vec{\lambda}$ and $\vec{V_i} - \vec{W_i}$ are short.

LFSR (Linear Feedback Shift Register)

Definition

Fibonacci LFSR: Shift left and XOR the "taps" to get the new rightmost element.

Galois LFSR: Work in Galois Field $\mathbb{F}_{2^n}$. Multiply by $x$ essentially.
