Macau Regional 2021

A: Given 2D grid of permutation, find a Hamilton walk that $\#\{A_i \lt A_{i+1}\} \le \#\{A_i \gt A_{i+1}\}$.

B: Construct a regular expression of ( 0/1 || . || .* ) of length $N$, such that the cost is maximised for maximum/minimum strategy for $.*$ in $O(N^2)$.

C: Given $N$ points which form complete graph $K_N$. Remove points such that one can go from $(0, 0)$ to outside without touching any edge.

D: Ka SPFA.

E: Given permutation $\sigma \in S_n$. Find $\sum i \cdot \sigma^k(i)$ for different $k$. $O((N + Q) \sqrt{N})$.

F: Given an array $A$ of length $N$. Operation: $A_i$ decrease by $N - 1$ and other increase by $1$. Determine final result or never terminates.

G: Given an cyclic array $A$ of length $N$. Visit $A_i$ in increasing order by rotating element to $A_1$ to $A_K$.

H: For permutation $f(p) = \sum_{|p_i - p_{i+1}|}$. Sum $f$ over all permutations that is topological sort of a specific rooted tree. $O(N^3)$ with $0/1$.

I: Given $N$ strings (total length $\le 10^6$) as nodes, where edge weight is length of longest common substring. Find maximum total weight of spanning tree. Extended Suffix Automaton.

J: Given a tree of weighted edges initially with one node. Supports (1) Add node with colour and (2) Change node colour. Find diameter of between distinct colours. $O(Q \log Q)$.

K: Given a simple graph of edge $k$ weighted $2^k$. Find minimum cycle length.
