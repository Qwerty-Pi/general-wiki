Universal Cup 3 Stage 14: Harbin

A: Construct DFA of $\Sigma = \{0, 1\}$ to recognize $[L, R]$.

B: Find the maximum area of non-convex polygon.

C: Transform instructions of directions ($\texttt{N, S, W, E}$) to relative movements ($\texttt{Z, L, R}$).

D: Given a $2 \times N$ grid of characters. Find down-left (sub-)path that is double string. Harmonic string matching.

E: Knapsack add and subtraction in $O(k)$.

F: Given $N$ objects with weights and discrete attractions. Simulate the events.

G: Print a connected graph.

H: Compute $\prod_{i = 0}^{L - 1} M_{i \cdot d \mod L}$ where $M$ consists of at most $N$ ($\le 2 \times 10^3$) segments.

I: Construct a hypercube where $\sum b_i = S$ and $\prod b_i = M$ ($\le 10^{10}$), and edit distance from $\{a_i\}$ is minimal.

J: Maintain a priority queue.

K: Calculate some contribution and binary search.

L: Define $P$ be the set of all paths of a tree. Find $\mathbb{E}[\sum_{p,q \in P} |p \cap q|^2]$.

M: Calculate $\frac{a}{c}$ where $c \mid a$ and $c \le b$ and no $c \lt x \le b$ such that $x \mid a$.
