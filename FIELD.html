Problems

Problem: Given two polynomial $f, g \in \mathbb{Z}[x]$. Find $x \in \mathbb{Z}$ such that $\gcd(f(x), g(x)) > 1$.

Solution: Euclidean Algorithm till the last step ($f$ linear, $g$ constant) or resultant.

Problem: Factor $n$ in form of $p \cdot (p^2 + p + 1) \cdot r$.

Solution: Consider arbitrary $a \in \mathbb{Z}_{p^3}$. Then $a^n \in \mathbb{Z}_p$, and so $p \mid [x] a^n$.
